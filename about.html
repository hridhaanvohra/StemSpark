StemSpark

StemSpark is a non-profit initiative designed to engage students in STEM through fun and interactive lessons. Offering a range of courses in areas such as coding, video and photo editing, 3D design, and math, StemSpark aims to spark curiosity and foster a love for learning in these fields. Its goal is to make STEM accessible and exciting for students through online education.
